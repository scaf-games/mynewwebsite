About Us

S.I.H.S.S (Saqafathul Islam Higher Secondary School), located in Ummathur, Kerala, is dedicated to shaping the leaders of tomorrow. With a strong commitment to both academic excellence and moral integrity, we have been educating young minds since 1980. Our mission is to foster an environment of learning and growth, where students are encouraged to explore, create, and achieve their full potential.

Our faculty is comprised of experienced educators, who are passionate about teaching and nurturing students. With state-of-the-art facilities, modern classrooms, and a focus on holistic development, we strive to prepare students for the challenges of the future.
